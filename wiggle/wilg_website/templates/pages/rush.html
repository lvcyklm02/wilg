What's the difference between a member and a boarder?

Both are residents of the house, however their involvement in the community differ slightly. Members attend house meetings twice a month and help run the house. Together, they make up the executive board and also organize and run events like rush, social events and a retreat every semester. Boarders are students who live in the house for a semester/year. All residents are responsible for two hours of chores a week.
