Our House

Ammenities

We house 45 residents in singles, doubles, and triples across 5 floors.

WILG has many amenities: a full commercial kitchen, a secondary kitchen on the fourth floor, ample appliances and dishware, wifi, free laundry, a bike storage room, and air-conditioned lounges.

Location

We're located conveniently between Central Square and Kendall Square, WILG offers quick access to the best of Cambridge and Boston.

  • 0 seconds from the 1 Bus stop
  • 1 minute from 3 BlueBike stations
  • 6 minutes from the Central Square Red Line station
  • 10 minutes from the MIT campus
  • 15 minutes from Kendall Square

We have easy access to grocery stores, restaurants, and MIT and Harvard campuses.

We have a beautiful mural on the wall of the house, painted by IMAGINE @imagine864.

House Rules

All residents commit to keeping the house clean and tidy, with two hours of chores a week.

We have a strict no-mammalian-pet policy, due to allergies.

Photo by Oron Nomboor @grain_and_pixel, Feb 2024

Food

Our pantries and beverage fridge is always stocked with snacks from Costco, and we take turns cooking dinner, so that six days a week, our members and boarders are guaranteed a home-cooked meal.

Tour

We're happy to give tours of the house to anyone interested in joining the community. Please email us at wilg-rush-chairs@mit.edu to schedule a tour.
