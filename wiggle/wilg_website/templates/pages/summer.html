SUMMER 2024 INFO:

Located conveniently between Central Square and Kendall Square, WILG offers quick access to the best of Cambridge and Boston. The 1 Bus stops directly in front of the house, there are 3 BlueBike stations across the street, the Central Square Red Line station is 6 minutes away, and Kendall Square is a 15 minutes away. We have easy access to grocery stores, restaurants, and MIT and Harvard campuses.

WILG has many amenities: a full commercial kitchen, a secondary kitchen on the fourth floor, ample appliances and dishware, wifi, free laundry, a bike storage room, and air-conditioned lounges.

During the summer, WILG is open to people of all sexes and genders and non-MIT students as well. Join us!

Dates

For summer 2024, we will offer housing from Sunday, June 2nd to Saturday, August 24th.

Pricing

These prices are for the full term: 06/02 - 08/24.

Room Price (Per Person)
Walk-through Triple* $2900
Double $3000

*A walk-through room is a set of two rooms connected by a door and shared by 2 or 3 people; to access the back room one must walk through the front room. The bed arrangement for these rooms is left up to the residents but a common set up is one bed in the front room and one (or two for a walk-through triple) in the back room. Another common arrangement is all beds in the back room and desks in the front room. Living in one of these is like having roommates, but you still get your own space.

Subletting is not allowed. These rates cannot be prorated unless another boarder has complementary summer plans and the arrangement is discussed with the summer housing chair in advance.

Details

Each person living at WILG will do a house and kitchen job each week (approx 1 hour each) to help keep the house clean and nice to live in. In addition to these jobs, each person is required to complete 3 bathroom cleanings over the course of the summer. Each missed job results in a fine of up to $15.

Each resident is required to sign a summer housing contract and submit a summer housing deposit ($500) to secure their spot. On top of the summer rate, an additional $300 security deposit is required upon move-in. The security deposit will be returned to the resident upon move-out minus any fines the resident has accrued from missed jobs. Additional fines will be taken from the security deposit if the resident's room and furniture is not left in good condition.

We have a limited number of parking spaces behind our building. Depending on requests, we may be able to grant permanent parking spaces for the summer but they are typically left open for temporary resident parking. You can also apply for an MIT parking permit.
